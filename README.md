# Learning Tracker — Timur Siraziev

A personal progress tracker built to document my transition from 25 years in broadcast journalism into humanitarian data analysis.

## What this is

A single-page web app that tracks my learning journey from June to December 2026, when I complete my Level 4 Data Analyst Apprenticeship with Corndel and Imperial College London.

No backend. No login. Everything runs in the browser and saves to localStorage.

## Why I built this

I believe that how you learn is as important as what you learn. This tracker keeps me accountable, documents my progress, and gives potential employers a transparent view of my development journey.

## What's inside

**Dashboard**
- Countdown to Level 4 graduation (December 2026)
- Weekly progress counters: SQL tasks, Power BI exercises, job applications
- October Readiness Checklist — 10 skills I must demonstrate before applying

**Monthly Plan**
- 7-month roadmap from SQL foundations to EPA submission
- Task-level tracking with visual progress bars
- Each task can be marked complete

**Learning Journal**
- Daily notes in English
- Tagged by topic: SQL, Power BI, Python, Excel, Interview Prep, Applications
- Chronological log of what I learn, what's hard, what clicks
